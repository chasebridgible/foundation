Send Arrival Update Job

Keep the customer informed when technician arrival changes.

Capability supported

Supports Protect customer relationship.

Current handling

Customer coordination watches route updates and sends customer messages when timing changes.

ActorRole
Customer coordinatorOwns intake, scheduling, customer updates, and review requests.
TechnicianCompletes work, records job evidence, and communicates field status.

Process

  1. Read current route and technician status.
  2. Compare current ETA to promised window.
  3. Send arrival update when timing changes materially.
  4. Record the sent message on the service job.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Customer messagingConfirmations, arrival updates, issue recovery, and review requests.
Route boardTechnician assignments, geography, arrival estimates, and route load.

Evidence and evaluation

A sent arrival message tied to route state proves the customer was updated.
