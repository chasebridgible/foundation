Book Service Appointment Job

Offer and confirm a customer appointment window that can be dispatched.

Capability supported

Supports Schedule service work.

Current handling

The customer coordinator receives request details and owns the customer confirmation.

ActorRole
Customer coordinatorOwns intake, scheduling, customer updates, and review requests.

Process

  1. Review triage record and customer constraints.
  2. Offer available windows with service category context.
  3. Confirm appointment and customer expectations.
  4. Send appointment confirmation and preserve the message record.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
CRM schedule boardCustomer record, service category, appointment window, and job status.
Customer messagingConfirmations, arrival updates, issue recovery, and review requests.

Evidence and evaluation

A confirmed CRM appointment and sent customer confirmation prove the visit is scheduled.
