Close Work Order Job

Review completion evidence and close or reopen the service job.

Capability supported

Supports Deliver service visit.

Current handling

The service manager checks completion state before the job is released to billing.

ActorRole
Service managerOwns quality recovery, priority rules, and daily capacity tradeoffs.

Process

  1. Review technician notes and blocked states.
  2. Confirm customer-facing outcome and billable amount.
  3. Close the work order or reopen with an owner.
  4. Release closed jobs to invoicing.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Mobile work order appTechnician checklist, photos, parts, signatures, and completion notes.
CRM schedule boardCustomer record, service category, appointment window, and job status.

Evidence and evaluation

A closed service job with billable approval proves the work can move to payment.
