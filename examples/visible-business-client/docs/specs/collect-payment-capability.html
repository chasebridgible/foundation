Collect Service Payment Capability

Completed work turns into collected cash or a named open balance with an accountable owner.

Outcome contract

Completed work turns into collected cash or a named open balance with an accountable owner.

  • Invoices must trace back to a closed work order and approved amount.
  • Receipts must trace back to the customer, service job, invoice, and payment method.
  • Refund and open-balance exception ownership stays visible until assigned.

Evidence and evaluation

Days to collect: Most completed jobs are collected before the next business day.

  • Issued invoice proves Create service invoice.
  • Paid invoice receipt proves Collect service payment.
  • Payment exception resolution proves Resolve payment exception.
