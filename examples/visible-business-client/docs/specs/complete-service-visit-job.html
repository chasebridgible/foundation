Complete Service Visit Job

Perform the promised service and record the customer-visible outcome.

Capability supported

Supports Deliver service visit.

Current handling

The technician performs the visit, records work performed, and notes any incomplete follow-up.

ActorRole
TechnicianCompletes work, records job evidence, and communicates field status.

Process

  1. Arrive and confirm service scope.
  2. Complete work or record blocked reason.
  3. Capture materials, notes, and customer-facing outcome.
  4. Send completion status or escalation note.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Mobile work order appTechnician checklist, photos, parts, signatures, and completion notes.
Customer messagingConfirmations, arrival updates, issue recovery, and review requests.

Evidence and evaluation

A completed work order with notes, materials, and outcome state proves service was performed or escalated.
