Schedule Service Work Capability

Customers receive a confirmed visit window with enough context for the business to dispatch the right technician.

Outcome contract

Customers receive a confirmed visit window with enough context for the business to dispatch the right technician.

  • Every confirmed appointment must have a customer, address, service category, preferred window, and priority.
  • Emergency priority is visible as a named gap until the owner defines its override rules.
  • Scheduling is not complete until the customer receives confirmation.

Evidence and evaluation

First available appointment window: Same-day or next-business-day window is visible before confirmation.

  • Request triage record proves Triage service request.
  • Appointment confirmation proves Book service appointment.
  • Dispatch assignment proves Dispatch technician.
