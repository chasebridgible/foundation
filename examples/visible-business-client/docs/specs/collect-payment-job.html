Collect Service Payment Job

Collect payment and record receipt or open balance.

Capability supported

Supports Collect service payment.

Current handling

The billing coordinator owns payment follow-up and receipt recording.

ActorRole
Billing coordinatorOwns invoices, payment follow-up, receipts, and open balance exceptions.

Process

  1. Confirm invoice is ready to collect.
  2. Charge saved card or send payment link.
  3. Record receipt when payment succeeds.
  4. Create aging exception when payment fails or remains open.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Payment systemInvoices, payment links, receipts, refunds, and aging status.
Customer messagingConfirmations, arrival updates, issue recovery, and review requests.

Evidence and evaluation

A paid invoice receipt or aging exception proves collection state.
