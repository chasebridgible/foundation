Field Service Business System

Brightline Home Services is a fictional residential field service business. The system must reliably receive customer requests, schedule work, dispatch and prepare technicians, complete service visits, collect payment, protect customer relationships, and keep technician capacity ready.

Operating loop

  1. Request enters triage.
  2. Appointment is booked and dispatched against capacity.
  3. Technician prepares, completes, and closes the visit.
  4. Billing invoices and collects payment or names an exception owner.
  5. Customer communication and technician readiness evidence feed the owner review loop.

Evidence and revision

The graph must expose the business as a connected operating system: capabilities connect to jobs, jobs connect to process, actors, tools, evidence, metrics, and gaps, and technical/eval specs link back to what they support or evaluate.
