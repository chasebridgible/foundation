Create Service Invoice Job

Turn a closed work order into a customer invoice with traceable line items.

Capability supported

Supports Collect service payment.

Current handling

The billing coordinator confirms billable scope, tax, discounts, and customer payment method.

ActorRole
Billing coordinatorOwns invoices, payment follow-up, receipts, and open balance exceptions.

Process

  1. Review closed work order and approved amount.
  2. Create invoice lines and taxes.
  3. Attach service job reference.
  4. Send invoice or payment link to customer.

ToolUse in job
Payment systemInvoices, payment links, receipts, refunds, and aging status.

An issued invoice linked to the closed service job proves billing is ready.
