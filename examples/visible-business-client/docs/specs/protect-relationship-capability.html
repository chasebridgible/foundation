Protect Customer Relationship Capability

Customers know what is happening, can recover from service issues, and are asked for feedback at the right time.

Outcome contract

Customers know what is happening, can recover from service issues, and are asked for feedback at the right time.

  • Arrival updates must be based on current route state, not stale appointment assumptions.
  • Service issues need a named owner, recovery promise, and follow-up evidence.
  • Review requests are sent only after the job is closed and no unresolved issue is open.

Jobs

JobActorOutcome
Send arrival updateCustomer coordinatorKeep the customer informed when technician arrival changes.
Handle service issueService managerOwn customer complaints, callbacks, damage reports, and incomplete work until recovery is clear.
Request customer reviewCustomer coordinatorAsk satisfied customers for a review without contacting unresolved issue cases.

Evidence and evaluation

Issue recovery time: Service issues receive owner response before the next service day.

  • Arrival message log proves Send arrival update.
  • Service recovery case proves Handle service issue.
  • Review request log proves Request customer review.
