Dispatch Technician Job

Assign technician capacity and route context to confirmed service work.

Capability supported

Supports Schedule service work.

Current handling

The dispatcher owns the assignment and the technician acknowledges the route.

ActorRole
DispatcherTurns scheduled work into technician-ready route assignments.
TechnicianCompletes work, records job evidence, and communicates field status.

Process

  1. Review confirmed appointments.
  2. Match skill, location, and availability.
  3. Assign route and arrival expectation.
  4. Notify technician and record acknowledgment.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Route boardTechnician assignments, geography, arrival estimates, and route load.
Technician skill rosterSkill, license, territory, capacity, and PTO constraints.

Evidence and evaluation

A route assignment and technician acknowledgment prove the job is operationally ready.
