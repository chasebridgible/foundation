Restock Technician Truck Job

Replenish critical truck materials before they block customer work.

Capability supported

Supports Maintain technician readiness.

Current handling

The inventory coordinator reviews usage and low-stock signals from technician work orders.

ActorRole
Inventory coordinatorKeeps technician trucks stocked and records material exceptions.
TechnicianCompletes work, records job evidence, and communicates field status.

Process

  1. Review parts used and low-stock truck signals.
  2. Pick materials for truck replenishment.
  3. Record restock quantity and exceptions.
  4. Escalate missing critical parts before dispatch.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Truck inventory logTruck stock, parts usage, replenishment, and low-stock exceptions.
Mobile work order appTechnician checklist, photos, parts, signatures, and completion notes.

Evidence and evaluation

A restock confirmation or shortage exception proves truck readiness state.

Revision

Truck stock min/max rules need owner review
