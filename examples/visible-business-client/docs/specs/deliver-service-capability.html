Deliver Service Visit Capability

Technicians arrive prepared, complete the promised work, and produce enough evidence to close the work order.

Outcome contract

Technicians arrive prepared, complete the promised work, and produce enough evidence to close the work order.

  • A technician cannot start a visit without appointment context, customer notes, and expected materials.
  • Completion requires outcome notes, materials used, and customer-facing completion state.
  • Photo evidence standards are visible as a gap until the service manager defines them.

Evidence and evaluation

Same-day completion rate: Completed visits close or escalate before end of service day.

  • Completed work order proves Complete service visit.
