Resolve Payment Exception Job

Resolve failed, disputed, refunded, or aged payment states without losing accountability.

Capability supported

Supports Collect service payment.

Current handling

Billing owns the exception queue and routes disputed work to the service manager.

ActorRole
Billing coordinatorOwns invoices, payment follow-up, receipts, and open balance exceptions.
Service managerOwns quality recovery, priority rules, and daily capacity tradeoffs.

Process

  1. Review failed charge, dispute, refund, or aged invoice.
  2. Name the next owner and customer promise.
  3. Apply adjustment, retry, refund, or escalation.
  4. Close exception with reason and evidence.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Payment systemInvoices, payment links, receipts, refunds, and aging status.
CRM schedule boardCustomer record, service category, appointment window, and job status.

Evidence and evaluation

A closed payment exception with owner, reason, and resulting balance proves the issue is resolved.
