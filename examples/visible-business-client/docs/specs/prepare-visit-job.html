Prepare Service Visit Job

Review job context and required materials before arriving at the customer site.

Capability supported

Supports Deliver service visit.

Current handling

The technician checks route notes, customer constraints, and truck stock before the visit.

ActorRole
TechnicianCompletes work, records job evidence, and communicates field status.

Process

  1. Open assigned work order.
  2. Review service category, notes, and access instructions.
  3. Confirm critical materials are on the truck.
  4. Mark visit ready or raise a readiness exception.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
Mobile work order appTechnician checklist, photos, parts, signatures, and completion notes.
Truck inventory logTruck stock, parts usage, replenishment, and low-stock exceptions.

Evidence and evaluation

A readiness checklist tied to the work order proves the visit was prepared.
