Triage Service Request Job

Classify the inbound service request so the business knows whether to schedule, escalate, or reject it.

Capability supported

Supports Schedule service work.

Current handling

The customer coordinator owns inbound requests and asks enough questions to determine category, urgency, and location.

ActorRole
Customer coordinatorOwns intake, scheduling, customer updates, and review requests.

Process

  1. Capture customer identity and service address.
  2. Classify service category, urgency, and access constraints.
  3. Flag emergency or out-of-scope requests for owner decision.
  4. Pass schedulable requests to appointment booking.

Context, tools, and interfaces

ToolUse in job
CRM schedule boardCustomer record, service category, appointment window, and job status.
Customer messagingConfirmations, arrival updates, issue recovery, and review requests.

Evidence and evaluation

A triage record with category, priority, address, and next state proves the request is ready for scheduling.

Revision

Emergency priority rules need owner definition
