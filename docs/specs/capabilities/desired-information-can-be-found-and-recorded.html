Desired Information Can Be Found And Recorded

Desired information can be found and recorded with enough scope, source evidence, search history, and final status for another actor to trust and continue the work.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-20
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation should make research a reliable ability: desired information can be found, judged against scope, recorded with evidence, and shown in a useful form without depending on chat memory.

Interfaces and sources

  • foundation.research.eval defines acceptance evidence for source coverage, scope, dedupe, access readability, and publish state.
  • skills/research/SKILL.md is the agent procedure for running or implementing this capability.
