Failure Recovery Is Ready

Known failure modes have recovery paths, owners, evidence, user/operator communication, and revision targets before they are needed under pressure.

Status: draft
Updated: 2026-06-06
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation should make recovery clear enough that humans and agents can respond to system failures without rediscovering ownership, state, rollback, communication, evidence, or revision rules during the incident.
