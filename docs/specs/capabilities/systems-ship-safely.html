Systems Can Ship Safely

Systems can move from build-ready to live use with known checks, owners, environments, rollback paths, and review evidence.

Status: draft
Updated: 2026-06-06
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation should make shipping safe enough that humans and agents know what is being released, what environment it affects, which checks passed, what can be rolled back, and which decisions remain human-owned.
