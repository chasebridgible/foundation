Evaluate Quality and Completeness

This capability makes quality explicit by connecting checks, rubrics, eval specs, and revision queues.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-01
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation must distinguish structurally valid artifacts from semantically acceptable ones. Checks prove shape; evals judge completeness, source traceability, rebuild usefulness, graph usefulness, and revision readiness.
