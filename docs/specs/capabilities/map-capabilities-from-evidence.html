Map Capabilities From Evidence

This capability names what the business or system can reliably do, separate from the current implementation.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-01
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation must convert source-backed surfaces, functions, artifacts, and intake answers into a capability map that is stable enough for humans to inspect and precise enough for agents to use. The map separates outcomes from implementation details and tracks confidence, evidence, gaps, and split needs.
