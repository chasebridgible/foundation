Capabilities Become Workable Jobs

This capability makes capabilities executable by naming the work bodies, process steps, actors, handoffs, states, and evidence.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-01
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation must not stop at high-level capability labels. It must define the recurring bodies of work that make each capability real, including actors, process, context, rules, and evidence requirements.
