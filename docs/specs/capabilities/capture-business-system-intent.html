Capture Business and System Intent

This capability makes intake durable: transcripts, briefs, docs, notes, exports, and interviews become traceable answers and operating signals.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-01
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation must preserve what a business or system is trying to do before agents start drafting specs or changing code. Intake captures source provenance, answered and unanswered questions, confidence, freshness, and signals that can later become capabilities, jobs, actors, processes, tools, evidence, metrics, and gaps.

FieldContractEvidence or source
