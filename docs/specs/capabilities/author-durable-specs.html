Author Durable Specs

This capability turns reviewed intent and evidence into source-of-truth contracts that humans and agents can read.

Status: active
Updated: 2026-06-03
Canonical section: #capability-intent

Capability intent

Foundation must author specs that survive the current conversation. Durable specs align visible prose, spec metadata, graph metadata, coverage, owned paths, and registry entries.

Outcome contract

FieldContractEvidence or source
BeneficiaryFuture agents, reviewers, and clients inspecting the operating system.Foundation operating system and linked child specs.
Reliable outcomeSpecs are HTML-native, graph-compatible, source-linked, checked, and specific enough to drive work.Child job specs, graph metadata, and checks.
Domain objectSpec files, templates, registry entries, and authoring skills.Spec registry and graph metadata.
ConstraintsHigh-level specs own intent and child specs; low-level specs own paths, behavior, and coverage.Spec workflow, graph check, self-map check.
Failure modeDocs become a pile of prose that cannot drive implementation or evaluation.Missing capability ownership, stale specs, or unreviewed agent output.
