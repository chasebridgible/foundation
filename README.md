# Foundation

Foundation is a set of core concepts for building compounding systems.

The idea comes from my life as a Division 1 tennis player. The biggest thing that stopped me short of my ultimate goals was not understanding the foundations early enough. In tennis, learning the technical foundations early prevents debt: you avoid relearning basic mechanics later, and you reduce the risk of injuries created by bad patterns repeated under pressure.

The same pattern holds in every serious domain. The people who reach the pinnacle learn and follow the foundational principles and rules. They do not treat fundamentals as beginner material. They use them as the base layer for compounding systems that let them improve over time.

This repo is the written substrate for that idea.

## Thesis

Foundations prevent avoidable debt.

Technical debt, comprehension debt, process debt, and physical injury all rhyme: they come from repeated work on top of weak fundamentals. The earlier the foundation is learned, the more every later repetition compounds in the right direction.

The goal of this repo is to make those foundations explicit enough that people and agents can build from them without having to relearn the same lessons later.
