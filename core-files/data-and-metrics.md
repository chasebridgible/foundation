# Data and metrics

## Why this lane matters

This lane captures **what gets measured**, **what stays qualitative**, and **what signals feed decisions**. Without alignment here, dashboards mislead and AI lacks trustworthy grounding.

---

## Questions

1. **What decisions** do you make weekly or monthly—and **what evidence** do you actually use versus what you wish you had?

2. Which metrics do you **trust implicitly**, which do you **distrust**, and why?

3. What lives mainly as **qualitative judgment** today—relationship quality, craft quality, culture—and should it stay that way or become partially quantitative?

4. Where would **better quantitative discipline** change behavior fastest—and what would need to be measured consistently without adding bureaucracy?

5. What **signals are leading** versus **lagging** for your business—and where do you confuse activity with outcomes?

6. How clean is **historical data**—consistent definitions, timestamps, ownership—and where would analysis fail because records were never captured?

7. What **single dashboard or weekly snapshot**, if perfectly accurate, would make your leadership conversations materially easier?
